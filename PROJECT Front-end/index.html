Our vision is to use our creativity that comes with precision to voice out what we call FASHION.



Style is a way to say who you are without having to speak.

You can have anything you want in life if you dress for it.

Slimz is a fashion brand that targets the exquisiteness in design and delivers high standards to people of its class. The brand takes
a look at the iconic fashion trends in the 70s and modifies it into a unique entity thereby creating an outstanding look for its customers.

Slimz Clothings are crafted with accuracy in measurement and delivers good finishing. Slimz portrays power in a woman, modesty, class and style. It tells a story about African heritage thereby using contemporary fabrics with an African accessory.
